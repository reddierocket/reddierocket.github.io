Fluent Korean in 1 Year

A step-by-step plan to reach Korean fluency in 12 months.

Month 1 — August 4

  • Learn Korean writing (Hangul)
  • Learn 30 survival phrases (ordering food, weather, directions, etc.)
  • Write 3 Korean sentences per day

Months 2–3 — September 4

  • Learn 5 new words daily
  • Review all words on flashcards
  • Write 3–5 diary sentences daily
  • Read 1 short webtoon chapter or article daily

Months 4–6 — November 4

  • Continue previous tasks
  • Listen to a 5-minute podcast daily
  • Say 5 sentences from podcasts, TTMIK, etc.
  • Record a 1-minute voice note daily

Months 7–9 — February 4

  • Continue previous tasks
  • Use a TTMIK structured dialogue, read along and mimic a full conversation
  • Increase diary entries to 5–10 sentences daily
  • Increase daily recordings to 2 minutes

Months 10–12 — May 4

  • Continue previous tasks
  • Watch Korean content without subtitles
  • Write diary entries without looking anything up
  • Chat with a Korean chatbot for at least 5 minutes daily
  • Think in Korean during activities
