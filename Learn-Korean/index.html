Fluent Korean in 1 Year

Plan Overview

Follow this month-by-month roadmap to build fluency in Korean within one year.

August (Month 1)

  • Weeks 1–2: Learn Korean writing
  • Weeks 3–4: Learn 30 survival phrases (order food, weather, directions, etc.)
  • Weeks 3–4: Write 3 Korean sentences per day

September (Month 2)

  • Learn 5 new words daily
  • Review all words with flashcards
  • Write 3–5 sentences in diary
  • Read 1 short webtoon chapter or 1 article

November (Month 3)

  • Learn 5 new words daily
  • Review all words with flashcards
  • Write 3–5 sentences in diary
  • Read 1 short webtoon chapter or 1 article
  • Listen to a 5-minute podcast
  • Say 5 sentences from podcast, TTMIK, etc.
  • Record a 1-minute voice note

February (Month 6)

  • Use structured dialog in TTMIK, reading & mimicking full conversation
  • Increase diary to 5–10 sentences
  • Increase daily recordings to 2 minutes

May (Month 9)

  • Watch Korean content on Netflix/YouTube with no subtitles
  • Write diary entries without looking things up
  • Chat with Korean chatbot for 5 minutes
  • Think in Korean while doing activities
